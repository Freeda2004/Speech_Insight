About the Application

Speech Insight is an academic prototype designed to demonstrate how speech and text can be jointly analyzed to enhance natural language understanding. The system integrates automatic speech recognition with punctuation restoration, prosodic stress cues, and syllable-level temporal modeling. Although multiple modalities are accepted as input, the punctuation restoration module is driven primarily by speech-based modeling to highlight the power of acoustic cues in language structure.
